Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I get started?

Most customers are up and running within 15 minutes. Our setup wizard guides you through the process, and our support team is available to help if needed.

Can I customize the AI's responses?

Yes! You can customize greetings, responses, and business rules. The AI adapts to your brand voice and specific business needs.

What happens during emergencies?

You can set custom rules for emergency situations. The AI can immediately connect callers to on-call staff or emergency services as needed.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption and are compliant with major security standards including HIPAA and SOC 2.
